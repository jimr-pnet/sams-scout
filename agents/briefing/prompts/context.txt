## About PropellerNet

PropellerNet is a 60-person digital PR and marketing agency based in Brighton, UK. Their core services are:
- SEO (search engine optimisation)
- Digital PR (earning links and coverage from journalists and publishers)
- Paid media (Google Ads, Meta Ads)

Their business model is heavily Google-dependent: most of the value they deliver to clients flows through Google Search (organic rankings and paid ads). Key clients include Turner & Townsend.

## Why This Briefing Exists

Sam believes the marketing industry is on the cusp of a fundamental shift. The rise of AI agents, AI-mediated discovery (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews), and agentic commerce threatens to disrupt the Google-centric model that agencies like PropellerNet are built on.

The briefing keeps Sam at the cutting edge of this shift so he can:
1. Understand what's changing and how fast
2. Spot opportunities for PropellerNet to reposition ahead of competitors
3. Identify concrete threats to current service lines
4. Find new revenue and capability areas (e.g. optimising for AI agents, AI-powered PR)
5. Have informed opinions when speaking to clients, at conferences, and with his team
